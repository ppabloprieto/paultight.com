WHO WE ARE

Get to know about us

PAUL TIGHT started its operations in 2018 with its first investments in thematic mutual funds and first real estate acquisitions. Since then, it has broadened its portfolio and entered new markets.

Operation and management are currently focused on US financial markets and variable income equities, specializing in high-income dividend securities and sectors.

Within its alternative investments, PAUL TIGHT currently holds under management an international Real Estate portfolio with multiple properties spread across different locations.

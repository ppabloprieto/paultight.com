Our Story

PAUL TIGHT started operating in 2018 with initial ventures in multi-thematic funds and early RE acquisitions. Ever since, the boutique has broadened its portfolio and accessed new markets.

In 2023, following a major strategic overhaul, our operation and management were shifted towards US financial markets and equities, specializing in high-income securities and sectors.

During this time, the boutique decided to retain its previously consolidated RE assets as part of its alternative investment strategy, holding multiple properties across different international locations.
